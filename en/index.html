Samsung PSU Summary

  • Stock reward linked to long-term value growth (separate from OPI)
  • Stock payout based on 3-year price increase (3-year vesting)
  • Base Price: ~85,385 KRW (Set Oct 2025)
  • Payout Conditions (3-year Stock Increase)
    < 20%: No payout 20% to <40%: 50% payout 40% to <100%: 100% payout >= 100%: Up to 2x payout
  • Eligible: Samsung employees (CL1~CL4 levels)
